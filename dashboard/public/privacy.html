GymFlow Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: your name, phone number, email address, and appointment preferences. We also collect appointment history and session package details to manage your training schedule.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Schedule, confirm, and manage your training appointments
  • Send appointment reminders and confirmations via SMS
  • Track your session package balance
  • Communicate schedule changes or cancellations

3. SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your training appointments. Message frequency varies based on your schedule. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with service providers (such as our SMS delivery partner) solely to operate the scheduling service.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you are an active client. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting your trainer.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: privacy@gymflow.app
